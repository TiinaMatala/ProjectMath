Combinatronics is a branch of mathematics concerned about counting.

It has applications in graph theory, coding, cryptography and probability.

Combinatorics contains combinations and permutations. Permutations is used when you select objects and care about their order. Combinations is used when you do not care about the order.

Fibonacci Sequence

Fibonacci sequence in mathematics means that each number is the sum of the previous two numbers, starting from 0 and 1.

For example when choosing number of sequence to 5, you get 1,1,2,3,5. In some books, zero is left out of the sequence.

Applications of Fibonacci sequence include computer algorithms such as the Fibonacci heap data structure and the Fibonacci search technique and Fibonacci cubes used for interconnecting distributed and parallel systems.
